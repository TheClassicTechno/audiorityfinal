ABOUT OUR SOLUTION

Audiority is a machine learning mobile app developed specifically to detect outdoor background noises and notify hearing impaired users.

It uses the latest technology and is free, accessible, and accurate.

Watch Our Demo Video

WHAT AUDIORITY DOES

Detects Outdoor Sounds

Whether it's a moving vehicle or ambulance or an armed gunman, Audiority can help hearing impaired users move out of the way safely by utilizing their phones' microphones to pick up on these sounds - car horn, ambulance siren, and gunshot.

Displays Sounds on Screen

After detection of the three most potentially dangerous outdoor sounds, Audiority outputs the name of the sound directly on the screen for the user to identify. In addition, a picture representation of the sound is also displayed in case English is not the user's main language.

Records History of Sounds Detected

In addition to detecting and displaying the sounds detected, Audiority also gives the user an option to record the history of the sounds detected. Audiority users can see which specific sounds occurred and was identified at which exact time and date. This is important in case the user wants to report an accident, such as a car crash, or file a police report, such as for an armed person.

HOW AUDIORITY IS BUILT

CreateML

is the Apple framework I used for building, training, and optimizing Audiority's Audio Classification Model. It contains a custom deep convolutional neural network and is small enough (has a smaller number of parameters) to be integrated with and deployed into the iOS App. In addition, I used the Kaggle dataset UrbanSound8K (the sounds siren, gunshot, and car horn) to train my model on. The audio dataset is in the .wav format, including 929 siren sounds, 429 car horn sounds, and 374 gunshot sounds. I chose these sounds because they pose the most danger to people outside on the street.

Swift

is Apple's coding language I used for coding the UI of the mobile app, including its buttons.

XCode

is the Apple IDE I used to write Swift code in.

BENEFITS OF AUDIORITY

It's Free & Built Conveniently.

Because Audiority was developed with no extra overhead costs - just with a Mac computer and free software (XCode, Swift, CreateML), it will be available on the App Store as a free mobile app. Users just need to have an iPhone (1 billion users worldwide already own one). Because Audiority is built just using Apple's frameworks, integration, designing of the UI of the mobile app, and deployment of the machine learning model is fast and easy.

It's accessible & convenient to use.

With just 3 buttons to press, Audiority is a very straightforward and easy-to-use app. Users just need to press the start/stop recording button to have their phone's microphone start or stop detecting for sounds. Second, users can press the check/clear history button to record the time/date of a specific sound detected. And last of all, users can choose to have their phone detect one, two, or all 3 of the sounds at once.

It's accurate, reliable, & lasts forever.

My trained, edited, and optimized audio classification model can detect and classify siren, car horn, and gunshot sounds with a 90% accuracy each time. I have optimized the model enough that it could detect and output a sound category within approximately 1 second for the user. And because Audiority is an online app, it will not get damaged or deteriorate like a physical object (hearing aid) would.
